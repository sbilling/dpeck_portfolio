Dave Peck

View Resume

Dave Peck is a sports broadcaster from Old Lyme, CT, currently studying at Northwestern University’s Medill School of Journalism to earn his Master of Science with a focus in Sports Media. In the Summer of 2019, Dave worked as a sports intern for NBC 10 Boston where he gained experience in producing, editing and reporting.
Graduating from Bowdoin College in 2019, Dave studied history and theater as a way to use the liberal arts education to build the necessary skills for broadcasting. After competing on both the Bowdoin Varsity Football and Baseball teams, Dave gained experience in various broadcasting positions for the Bowdoin College Polar Bears and high school sport teams throughout Maine. He contributed as a broadcaster for Radio Midcoast WCME from 2016 to 2019 and served as the Sports Director of Bowdoin’s 91.1 WBOR station where he brought Bowdoin Athletics to the FM airwaves.
As a Polar Bear, Dave founded Bowdoin Polar Bear Nation, an Instagram account that gave voice to athletes and coaches through weekly interviews and feature segments. He also served as the play-by-play broadcaster for Bowdoin Baseball, calling games on both the Northeast Sports Network and the WBOR Sports Radio Network.
Dave worked for the Newport Gulls as Field Reporter and Color Commentator during the 2017 NECBL season and was promoted to Lead Broadcaster in the summer of 2018 where he called all 44 games for the Gulls.
During his 2018 semester in Rome, Dave worked with the S.S. Lazio Ducks American Football team as both a reporter intern and quarterback coach.
